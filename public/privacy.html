Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

1. Introduction

At AI Idea Refiner ("we", "us"), we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected and used.

2. Data Collection

Project ideas entered into our system are sent anonymously to Google (Gemini) or Groq servers for analysis. We do not store this data permanently or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies & Ads

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies to personalize ads. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
